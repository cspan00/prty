Q: What is prty.live

prty.live transforms your TV from a black mirror into a live social photo sharing platform. After you log in, you will be given a unique prty link that you can send to your friends. After your friends go to the link, they will be able to take pictures with their phones and send them to the screen

Q: Do I need a facebook account to use prty.live

Yes

Q: How do I use this?

prty.live is designed to be run on a laptop plugged into a television or monitor. Once you have created a new party and send out the link to your friends, set it and forget it! the Photos will start appearing on the screen as people at your party take them.

Q: Can I send X rated content to a prty as a guest?

Its your party, they are your friends. That is entirely your prerogative

Q: How do I toggle the menu while the prty is live?

By pressing the return key
